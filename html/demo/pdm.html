    Structure

    The PDM is sparated into four major stages

    Stage 1

    Inception

    The beginning of the project cycle which includes gaining stakeholder approval and project kick-off.

    Stage 2

    Construction

    Completing the design process including municipality approvals, purchasing all labor and materials, putting all the pieces together whilst ensuring safety.

    Stage 3

    Testing & Acceptance

    Following the right testing regime to ensure the facility functions as designed.

    Stage 4

    Transition

    Accommodating the customer, ensuring the hand-off to site operations is accurate and complete and that the concerns of the operations staff have been addressed.
