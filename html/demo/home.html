About Us

This global project delivery model (PDM) has been developed by taking input from design and construction management in all three global regions, defining and agreeing all of the common and required elements and fundamental requirements.

Project Charter

What

Formalize a unified approach to deliver all CapEx Expansion projects from project Inception through Operationalisation of the facility/phase.

Why

Orderly execution of all project activities. Manage risks consistently (schedule, cost, and performance / quality). Comprehensively align teams (PARIS Matrix). Especially to define and manage H&S effectiveness across all projects

Principles

Simple, lightweight framework, sustainable and 100% buy-in of regions. Continual Improvement measured via KPIs Aligned with Global Design Standards, Equipment Specifications and processes, Reference Design.

How

Utilize a phase/-gate model identifying key sequences, objectives and deliverables, with the use of a standard tool-kit.

Best Practice

Adopts existing standards and best practices from both within Equinix and industry at large.

Standard Nomenclature

Aligns team members and promotes consistency by using standardised gates, deliverables and tool-kit.

Principles and Tools

Not intended to replace working level customisation but identifies the minimum deliverables and provides the standard tool-kit to realize the deliverables.

Measurement

Measure the effectiveness and adherence through implementation of regular and formal KPI reporting.
