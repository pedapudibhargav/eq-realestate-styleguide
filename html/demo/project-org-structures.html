The PDM is sparated into four major stages

Project Organization Structure – Global

  1. CPM is accountable for overall delivery of the project.

  2. Design Manager responsible for all design actions and deliverables.

  3. Engineering Consultants report to both CPM and DM. Formal day-to-day engagement is with DM and design team.

  4. General/Main Contractor works solely at the direction of the CPM and directly manages subcontractors.

  5. Site Operations works in an advisory and approvals capacity as the client/end user.
